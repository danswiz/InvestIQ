Claude Cowork

Overview

Claude Cowork is a research preview from Anthropic that brings Claude Code's agentic capabilities to the Claude Desktop app for non-coding knowledge work. It enables Claude to actively manage files, use tools, and browse the web to complete tasks rather than just discussing them.

Key Capabilities

  • File Management: Organize, rename, and sort local folders (Downloads, Documents, etc.)
  • Data Extraction: Turn screenshots, receipts, or invoices into formatted spreadsheets
  • Document Creation: Draft reports, presentations, and branded documents using company templates
  • Web & Cloud Integration: Works across the web via Chrome connector for research and data entry
  • Multi-Platform Connectors: Connects to Slack, Notion, GitHub, and other tools
  • Proactive Work: Generate daily briefings by synthesizing info across multiple platforms

How It Works

  1. Describe Your Goal: Tell Claude what outcome you want
  2. Grant Access: Choose which folders and connectors Claude can access
  3. Claude Executes: Claude breaks tasks into steps and works through them
  4. Approval Controls: Claude shows you plans and waits for approval before significant actions
  5. Review & Iterate: Redirect, refine, or take a different approach at any step

Use Cases

  • Administrative: Create daily briefings from Slack, Notion, and GitHub
  • Research & Analysis: Market sizing with professional deliverables (PowerPoint, Excel)
  • Sales & GTM: Aggregate customer feedback across platforms
  • Legal & Compliance: Manage and organize legal documents
  • Personal Productivity: Organize Downloads folder, rename files, extract data from receipts

Privacy & Security

  • Runs in an isolated virtual machine (VM) on your Mac
  • Conversation history stored locally, not on Anthropic's servers
  • Explicit permission required for folders and connectors
  • You control network access through an allowlist in Settings

Platform Requirements

  • Platform: macOS only (during research preview)
  • Access: Open Claude Desktop app, click "Cowork" in sidebar
  • Availability: Available to Pro subscribers and above

Current Limitations

  • Does not support Projects, Memory across sessions, Artifacts, or Google integrations
  • Sessions don't sync across devices
  • Cowork activity not captured in Audit Logs, Compliance API, or Data Exports (Enterprise)
  • Not recommended for HIPAA, FedRAMP, or FSI regulated workloads

Getting Started

  1. Download the Claude Desktop app for macOS
  2. Click "Cowork" in the sidebar
  3. Connect your files and tools
  4. Describe what you want to achieve
